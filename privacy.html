Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 04/13/2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the Boycott Palestine Extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your information while using the extension. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the data you share with us.

2. Data Collection

The Boycott Palestine Extension does not collect any personal data from users. The extension only compares data with a locally stored script (bundled with the extension) to determine whether a visited website is on a predefined list. The extension operates completely offline and does not access or store any user data, including browsing history or personal information.

3. Data Access

The extension only has access to the hostname of the websites you visit. It checks the hostname against a local script to determine if it matches any predefined boycott criteria. No other data is collected or transmitted outside of your device.

4. Data Usage

The extension uses the hostname data to display a notification when you visit a website that is on the boycott list. The data is processed locally on your device, and no data is shared with any third-party services.

6. Third-Party Access

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there are no specific rights you need to manage or revoke. However, you can choose to uninstall the extension at any time.
